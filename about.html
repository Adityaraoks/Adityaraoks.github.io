About Me

I am a Scientist (Sr. Scale) at the Division of Agriculture Economics, ICAR-Indian Agricultural Research Institute, New Delhi. I have a Ph.D. in Economics and Governance of Agriculture, Food and Natural Resources from Humboldt Universität zu Berlin, Germany. My research focuses on natural resource economics, agricultural policy evaluation, and the Water-Energy-Food nexus in India.

At the Indian Agricultural Research Institute, I teach courses on Micro-Economics, Green Economics, and Natural Resource Economics for Masters and Ph.D. students. I am passionate about sustainable agricultural systems and exploring innovative approaches to address challenges in the agricultural sector.

Research Interests

  • Adoption and impact of agricultural technologies
  • Sustainable transition of food systems
