Aditya Korekallu Srinivasa

Scientist (Senior Scale) | ICAR- Indian Agricultural Research Institute

I am a Scientist at ICAR- Indian Agricultural Research Institute, New Delhi. I have a Ph.D. in Economics and Governance of Food, Agriculture, and Natural Resources from Humboldt Universität zu Berlin, Germany. I am working evaluation of agricultural policies like Crop Insurance, Minimum Support prices and Agricultural credit using rigorous mixed methods research designs. I am also working on (Scarce)Water- (Clean) Energy – Food nexus in India. At Indian Agricultural Research Institute, I teach courses on Econometrics (Basics and Advanced), and Natural Resource Economics for masters and Ph.D. students.
