Teaching

At the Indian Agricultural Research Institute (ICAR-IARI), New Delhi, I am committed to training and mentoring the next generation of agricultural economists and researchers. My teaching emphasizes analytical rigor, practical application, and policy relevance.

Courses Taught

  • Econometrics (Basic and Advanced)
    Fundamentals and advanced techniques of econometric analysis, including regression, estimation, hypothesis testing, and applied econometric modeling for agricultural research. Taught for both Masters and Ph.D. students.
  • Natural Resource Economics
    Economic principles and policy issues related to sustainable management of natural resources, with a focus on water, land, and energy in Indian agriculture.
  • Agricultural Policy Analysis
    Design, implementation, and impact assessment of agricultural policies, including crop insurance, minimum support prices, and credit systems, using theoretical and empirical approaches.

Supervision & Mentoring

I supervise Masters and Ph.D. students on topics related to agricultural policy, resource economics, and the water-energy-food nexus, and contribute to curriculum development and academic workshops.
