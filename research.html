Research Themes and Approach

Agricultural Policy Evaluation

My research focuses on evaluating the impact and effectiveness of agricultural policies in India, with particular emphasis on:

  • Crop Insurance: Analyzing adoption patterns, willingness to pay, and impact of weather-based crop insurance schemes.
  • Minimum Support Prices (MSP): Examining awareness, impact on crop diversification, and effects on farm-gate prices.
  • Agricultural Credit: Evaluating institutional credit access, Kisan Credit Card schemes, and their impact on farm households.

This research stream aims to provide evidence-based insights for policy refinement and development to better support agricultural communities across India.

Water-Energy-Food Nexus

Investigating the critical interconnections between scarce water resources, clean energy solutions, and food production systems in India. My work in this area includes:

  • Solar-Powered Irrigation Systems: Examining stakeholder perceptions, adoption challenges, and policy preferences.
  • Irrigation Development: Analyzing distribution patterns, equity implications, and sustainable water management.
  • Micro-irrigation: Studying adoption trends, distributional patterns, and potential correlates.

This research contributes to understanding and addressing the complex challenges at the intersection of water management, renewable energy adoption, and food security in India.

COVID-19 Impact on Food Security

Examining the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic on food security and agricultural systems in India, with a focus on:

  • Food insecurity dynamics in rural areas during the pandemic
  • Vulnerability factors affecting household food security
  • Policy responses and intervention strategies

This work provides insights into the resilience of food systems during crisis situations and informs future policy preparedness.
