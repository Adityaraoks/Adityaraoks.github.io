About Me

I am a Scientist (Sr. Scale) at the Division of Agriculture Economics, ICAR-Indian Agricultural Research Institute, New Delhi. I hold a Ph.D. in Economics and Governance of Agriculture, Food and Natural Resources from Humboldt Universität zu Berlin, Germany.

My research focuses on natural resource economics, agricultural policy evaluation, and the Water-Energy-Food nexus in India. I am passionate about sustainable agricultural systems and exploring innovative solutions for the sector.

At IARI, I teach courses on Micro-Economics, Green Economics, and Natural Resource Economics for Masters and Ph.D. students.
